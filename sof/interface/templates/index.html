About Us

There are about 150 million farmers in India. Crop diseases are a major threat to food security, but their rapid identification remains difficult in many parts of the world due to the lack of necessary infrastructure. Improper care can lead to serious effects on plants and affects the quality, quantity or productivity of the yield.Thus,we came up with an approach to detect plant disease by uploading an image of the leaf.Along with this, Farmfully provides a platform where users can seek guidance from an expert.
